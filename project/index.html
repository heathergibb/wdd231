Alberta's Rocky Mountains are home to majestic mountain peaks, glacial lakes, spectacular valley and canyons, and abundant wildlife. Enjoy the beauty of the natural world while camping, hiking, climbing, skiing, and so much more.

Banff National Park

The natural beauty of Banff's surrounding lakes and mountains made it Canada's first National Park and the world's third national park refuge, and part of the Canadian Rocky Mountain Parks UNESCO World Heritage Site. More than 4 million people from around the world now visit Banff National Park every year.

Jasper National Park

Explore the largest national park in the Canadian Rockies! Summer or winter, there is always adventure and beauty to behold. Home of Miette Hot Springs, Maligne Valley, Edith Cavell, Athabasca Falls, and so much more.

Kananaskis Provincial Park

Located directly southeast of Banff National Park, Kananaskis country does not disappoint. Explore the breathtaking mountain scenery, an abundance of lakes, and a diversity of hikes. Just a short 1 hour drive from Calgary, Kananaskis is an excellent destination for an enjoyable day trip.
